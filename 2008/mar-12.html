Subscribing to RSS feed for a single phpbb forum

The forum for usage questions about nHibernate is a sub forum on the hibernate forum site. When you are in the nHibernate sub forum a nice and shiny RSS button is shown but the feed that it links to contains all items from all sub forums on the site which is not what i expected. I have lived with this for a while but today I got tired of it and did a little investigation. The forum runs on phpBB and a quick look around suggested that appending the forum identifier in the URL showed me only posts for this forum. I wonder why this is not part of the UI as its a rather nice feature to limit the amount of irrelevant information.

So in short to subscribe to only the nHibernate forum use this URL: http://forum.hibernate.org/rss.php?f=25
